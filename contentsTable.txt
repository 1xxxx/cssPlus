
- flexScaffolding.less
        (defines flexible elements like ROWS, COLS, CELL and their alignments);

- scaffoldingInlineMetrics.less
        (provides shortcuts like _FLEX, _150px or _60pct)

- scaffoldingCssMetrics.less
        (provides shortcuts for flexScaffolding.less classes like .cols() for .COLS and main-axis justification and cross-axis alignement)

- mediaQuery.less
        (defines media ranges and resolutions keywords like @mobile, @desktop but also device orientations and pixel density)
